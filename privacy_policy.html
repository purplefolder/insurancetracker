Privacy Policy – Insurance Tracker

Insurance Tracker respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Google account information (used for authentication only)
  • Vehicle information (plate number, engine number, renewal dates)
  • Insurance and bluebook details
  • Uploaded vehicle-related documents
  • Family member associations within the app
  • Reminder and notification preferences

How We Use Your Information

  • To manage vehicle records and renewal tracking
  • To send renewal reminders and notifications
  • To allow family-based vehicle management
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

Authentication

Insurance Tracker uses Google Sign-In (OAuth) for secure user authentication. We do not store user passwords.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is used only for the functionality of the app.

Data Security

We use appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and data at any time.

Account Deletion

Users can delete their account by contacting us. Once deleted, all associated data is permanently removed within 7 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
