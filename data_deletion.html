Delete Account – Insurance Tracker

This page explains how users of Insurance Tracker can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can delete your account in by the following way:

  • Send an email request from your registered Google account to: info@purplefolder.com.np

Data That Will Be Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • User account information (Google Sign-In identifier)
  • Vehicle details (plate number, engine number, renewal dates)
  • Insurance and bluebook renewal information
  • Uploaded documents
  • Family member and vehicle associations
  • Reminder and notification configurations

Data Retention

All personal and vehicle-related data is permanently deleted within 7 days of the deletion request. No personal data is retained after this period.

Contact

If you face any issues with account deletion, contact us at: info@purplefolder.com.np
